# Anti-AI patterns

Patterns to refuse, regardless of the user's custom anti-phrase list. These are AI tells that betray authorship even when individual words are clean.

## Structural forbids

**1. The "It's not X — it's Y" reframe.**
- Forbidden: "It's not about working harder — it's about working smarter."
- Why: signature ChatGPT pattern. Reads as essayed, not spoken.

**2. "Whether you're X or Y" false-inclusive openings.**
- Forbidden: "Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned pro..."
- Why: tries to address everyone, addresses no one.

**3. Triple-structure.**
- Forbidden: "It was bold. It was brave. It was necessary."
- Why: rhetorical scaffolding the user did not write themselves.

**4. Audience commands.**
- Forbidden: "Think about it." / "Picture this." / "Imagine..."
- Why: instructional voice, not conversational.

**5. Rhetorical profundity question at the end.**
- Forbidden: "What if the thing you've been chasing was inside you all along?"
- Why: pure AI ending energy.

**6. Consecutive paragraphs starting with the same word.**
- Forbidden: three paragraphs in a row that begin with "And" or "But" or "Because."
- Why: pattern repetition is a tell.

**7. Listicle framing in subject lines or openers.**
- Forbidden: "5 ways to grow your list" / "3 reasons why your emails flop"
- Allowed only when the user explicitly requests it.

## Em-dash rule

**Maximum 1 em-dash (—) in the body prose.** Headings, signature lines, and metadata don't count.

## Vocabulary forbids (the universal list)

These appear in nearly every "AI sounds like AI" complaint. Refuse outright:

- "Game-changer" / "game-changing"
- "Unlock" (as in "unlock your potential")
- "Empower" / "empowering"
- "Revolutionize" / "revolutionary"
- "Cutting-edge"
- "World-class"
- "Best-in-class"
- "Seamless" / "seamlessly"
- "Unprecedented"
- "Supercharge"
- "In today's fast-paced world"
- "At the end of the day"
- "Move the needle"
- "Take it to the next level"
- "Synergy"
- "Leverage" (as a verb)
- "Robust"
- "Holistic"
- "Curated"

The user's `anti-ai-phrases.md` extends this with their custom additions. Always read both.

## How to self-check

Before returning any draft, scan it for every item above. If any hits, rewrite that line. Never ship a draft that hits any item on this list.
