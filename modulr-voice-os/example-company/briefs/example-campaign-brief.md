# Campaign Brief — Spring Meridian Method Cohort

## Campaign overview

**Campaign name:** Spring 2025 Meridian Method Launch

**Duration:** 6 emails over 14 days

**Primary objective:** Enroll 30 women into the Spring 2025 Meridian Method cohort.

**Launch dates:** 2025-02-03 → 2025-02-14 (cohort starts 2025-02-17)

**Campaign positioning (one sentence):**
The 12-week program for women 35-55 who are ready to stop doing light-weight, high-rep workouts and actually get strong.

**Target audience segment:**
Both Segment A (Rebuilder) and Segment B (First-Timer) from `context/audience.md`. Myth-busting content hits harder for First-Timers; capability framing hits harder for Rebuilders.

---

## The one angle

**The core angle name:** The Myth-Bust + Capability Angle (combined)

**One-sentence articulation:**
The reason you haven't seen progress isn't that you're inconsistent — it's that you've been doing the wrong kind of training.

**Why this angle wins for this audience:**
1. It reframes prior "failure" as a programming problem, not a willpower problem (anti-shame)
2. It gives the reader a concrete next action (progressive overload) they can immediately visualize
3. It uses Sarah's clinical credibility to position Meridian as the science-backed alternative to wellness fluff

**The bridge statement (use verbatim across emails):**
"You're not broken. You're undertrained."

**What this campaign is NOT about:**
- Not about weight loss (never mention the scale)
- Not about "toning" (forbidden vocabulary — see `context/anti-ai-phrases.md`)
- Not a comparison to CrossFit / bodybuilding (stay in our lane)

## Constraints and rules

- **Do not use:** tone, sculpt, bikini body, drop X lbs, transform, bounce back, snap back, cheat day, detox, queen, crush it, let's go ladies
- **Must include in every email:** bridge statement "You're not broken. You're undertrained." (at least once across the campaign, not every single email)
- **Must mention offer:** soft mention in emails 1, 2, 4; direct CTA in emails 3, 5, 6
- **Unsubscribe risk:** medium — this list is hit with a weekly newsletter already. Don't over-send.

## Success metrics

- **Primary metric:** cohort enrollments
- **Target:** 30 enrollments
- **Secondary metrics:** open rate ≥30%, CTR ≥4% on promo emails, reply rate ≥5 per email
- **Decision gate:** if enrollments < 10 by end of day Feb 11, add a bonus (1:1 session upgrade) for the final 48 hours
