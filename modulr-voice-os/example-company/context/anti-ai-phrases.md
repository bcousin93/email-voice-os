# Anti-AI Phrases — Meridian Strength Coaching

## Universal forbidden phrases (see main anti-ai-phrases.md)

All universal patterns apply: "unlock your potential," "game-changer," "in today's fast-paced world," "dive in," em-dash overuse, "It's not X — it's Y" reframes, triple structures, audience commands ("Think about it"), "Whether you're X or Y," etc.

## Meridian-specific forbidden phrases

### Fitness/wellness clichés
- "Crush it"
- "Let's go, ladies"
- "Queen"
- "Girl boss"
- "Strong is the new skinny"
- "Sweat is fat crying"
- "Nothing tastes as good as skinny feels"
- "Bounce back"
- "Get your body back"
- "Snap back"

- "Clean eating"

- "Ageless"

## Forbidden structural patterns

All universal structural patterns apply. Plus Meridian-specific:

- **Before/after photo framing in copy** (we don't do visual transformation language)
- **"In just X weeks"** (implies quick fix — we sell multi-year strength, not 30-day makeovers)
- **Scale-weight emphasis** (we don't talk about pounds lost — we talk about weight lifted)

## Replacements table

| Instead of | Use |
|---|---|
| "Tone your arms" | "Build upper-body strength" |
| "Crush your workout" | "Hit your lifts" or "Train today" |
| "Bikini body" | (cut entirely — not a concept we use) |
| "Get your body back" | "Rebuild your strength" |
| "In just 8 weeks" | "Over the 8-week program" |

| "Detox" | (cut entirely — your liver already does this) |
