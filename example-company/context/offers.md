# Offers — Meridian Strength Coaching

## Offer 1: The Meridian Method (flagship program)

**One-sentence description:**
A 12-week progressive strength training program for women 35-55, delivered online with weekly coaching calls, a custom lifting program, and ongoing form reviews.

**Price:** $1,800 for 12 weeks, or 3 payments of $650

**What's included:**
- Customized 12-week progressive lifting program
- Weekly 60-minute group coaching call (live, recorded)
- 2 private 1:1 sessions with Sarah (start + midpoint)
- Form-check video reviews (submit as many as you want)
- Private Slack community of program members
- Nutrition guidance (protein targets and recovery, not diet plans)

**Who it's for:**
Segment A (The Rebuilder) and Segment B (The First-Timer) from `audience.md`. Women 35-55 who want to build real strength with expert guidance.

**Who it's NOT for:**
- Women under 30 who don't have joint or hormone considerations (we're not the best fit)
- Anyone looking to lose a specific amount of weight (we don't coach for scale outcomes)
- Anyone looking for quick results (this is a 12-week program that builds the first year of what we hope is a decade of strength)

### Selling angles

**Angle A: The Capability Angle**
- When to use: when the reader is motivated by what their body can do, not how it looks
- Hook pattern: "pick up your grandkid" / "carry your own suitcase" / "deadlift your bodyweight"
- Proof point to lead with: Rachel's story (proof point 2)

**Angle B: The Longevity Angle**
- When to use: when the reader is motivated by fear of frailty or bone-density loss
- Hook pattern: "you don't have to accept losing strength with age"
- Proof point to lead with: sarcopenia data (proof point 4)

**Angle C: The Myth-Bust Angle**
- When to use: for first-timers or skeptics who have bought into "I don't want to get bulky"
- Hook pattern: "five-pound dumbbells are not doing what you think they are"
- Proof point to lead with: Sarah's quote (proof point 3)

### Standard CTA language

- "Join the next Meridian Method cohort →"
- "See if the Meridian Method is right for you"
- "Book a 15-min fit call with Sarah"

**Never use:**
- "Transform your body"
- "Get the body you want"
- "Bikini body in 12 weeks"
- "Invest in yourself"
